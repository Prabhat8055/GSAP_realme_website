Realme Buds T300

The Realme Buds T300, exemplifying premium audio quality, boasts the cutting-edge Bluetooth version 5.3, ensuring a seamless and robust connection within a generous 10-meter range. Elevating the listening experience, the earbuds feature Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) with an impressive 30dB reduction, immersing users in their desired soundscape. The audio immersion is further amplified by massive 12.4mm sound drivers, delivering rich and detailed audio. Convenience meets longevity with a total playback time of 40 hours, encompassing both the earbuds and the case. Notably, a rapid 7-minute charge empowers the earbuds for an extended 7 hours of playback, showcasing the efficiency of its fast-charging capability.
